EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

Indie is a small company with big aspirations and we want you to be part of our diverse team

Sanlam Indie is a digital-first insurer, so we use smarter technology to give everyone a more personalised experience. We would really love for you to join us now to be part of the wonderful team at Indie.

Indie has a remote working culture.

Work from anywhere, work flexibly and do not get caught in nasty rush our traffic. That is life at Sanlam Indie.

Indie has a small team, with large ambitions.

Indie has a small team of less than 70 people making it less overwhelming to build new connections.

Indie uses cutting edge technology.

Technology helps use work faster and effeciently, making better use of your time and resources.

WHY SANLAM INDIE?

As a member of the Indie team, you’ll have the opportunity to play a key role in the transformation of the personal finance industry in South Africa. You’ll work with a group of smart, driven and fun people, in an environment where you’ll be given responsibility and freedom to do great work and solve really challenging problems.

  • We are a remote-friendly company. On Tuesdays and Thursdays, we get together at our office in Tygervalley to talk and work together; but the rest of the time can be spent where you do your best work. This means you’ll need to be good at communicating with people in your team.
  • We’re constantly building and improving our software. You’ll need to be adaptable and empathetic towards the people designing and using the software you create. Goalposts will shift, but you’ll have time to do good work, and the space to voice your opinions.
